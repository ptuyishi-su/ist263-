Over two-thirds of the world's wild tiger population is found in India. While you may have seen Bengal tigers in captivity in zoos throughout the world, seeing one wandering free in its natural habitat is a once-in-a-lifetime event. Corbett National Park, Ranthambore National Park, and the Pench Tiger Reserve are among the top reserves and national parks in India for a tiger safari.
